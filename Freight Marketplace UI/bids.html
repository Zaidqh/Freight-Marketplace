FAQ

What happens when I accept a quote?

The system creates a booking tied to the shipment and opens a message thread to coordinate pickup, paperwork, and delivery.

Can I edit or withdraw a quote?

In the MVP, quotes are immutable; you can submit another quote with updated details. In production, editing/withdrawing will be supported.

How do I add attachments?

Attachments are available within the Messages thread after a booking is created.

Policy

Fair bidding

Quotes should reflect true costs; no bait-and-switch pricing.

Insurance & compliance

Transporters must maintain proper insurance and comply with local laws.

Data privacy

Limit personal data in quotes; share sensitive details in the private message thread after booking.

Quote tips
  • Be explicit about included services (loading help, straps, blankets, tail-lift).
  • For cross-border moves, clarify if customs paperwork is included.
  • Offer windows for pickup/delivery to help scheduling.
  • Share prior lane experience to build trust.
